History & Introduction

A leading name within the industry, The Garden World is known for well-designed and constructed gardens, executed to the smallest detail and completed within schedule and budget. We can work for both established and newer up-and-coming garden designers and landscape architects, interpreting their designs and creating inspiring gardens. Over the years we have won several APL and Bali awards and have constructed Gold and Silver Gilt medal winning show gardens at the prestigious RHS Chelsea Flower Show. We pride ourselves in our professionalism, creativity and flexibility in creating gardens that provide our clients with attractive and enjoyable outdoor spaces.



Formed in 1998, the Garden World specialises in the design and construction primarily of private domestic gardens, both in the United Kingdom and overseas. Through the years, we have built a solid reputation also as garden consultants and for producing well-constructed gardens that meet and exceed our clients' expectations.

Between us, our management team has more than 55 years of experience in garden design, construction and horticulture. Our skills set covers all aspects of the business, enabling us to create genuinely impressive gardens.



The Garden World is based in Fulham, south-west London, in an office situated in a showcase/gallery for the best in exterior garden lighting, contemporary garden furniture, bespoke water features and exterior sculpture.
